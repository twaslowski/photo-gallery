far.

This is going to be a bit of a short edition – I'll just post what I have, and next week I'll be back with more.

We were received on Sunday evening by a colleague of Liza's, who showed us around.
Driving had been fairly stressful – we packed and got into our car on Sunday morning, but got into a few traffic
jams just outside of Dresden and ended up arriving at around 17:30. Given that we have to unpack all of our stuff,
take a walk with Panda and get to sleep early, 17:30 is pretty late.

In any case, the walk was quite nice. I don't have a lot of pictures of it – or of us – but I ended up getting
a few nice ones just outside the Dresden harbor area.

{{< carousel base="2026/06/dresden" images="dresden-hafen,dresden-kirche-1" alt="Admittedly, the light was great for these" >}}

recognize if you've spent any time there (or just looked at pictures of it).
